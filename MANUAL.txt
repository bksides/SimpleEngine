Objective:

Avoid letting the ball get past your paddle.  Maximize your score by
hitting the ball as many times as possible.  The ball and paddle
will both get faster as your score goes up.

Features:

 - Sweet music
 - Muting of sweet music (don't know why you'd want to do that, though)
 - Sweet sound effects
 - You can mute those too
 - Sick Pause Menu
 - Cool ass endgame screen that shows final score
 - Gravity (lame)
 - Green ball
 - No micro-transactions

Controls:

P: Pause the game
Enter: Start over(pause/game over only)
Arrow keys: Move the ball
M: Mute the music
S: Mute the sound effects
Page Up/Page Down: Control music volume
ESC: Exit the game
